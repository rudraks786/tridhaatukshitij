Welcome to the elevated lifestyle

p>There’s no place like home, and we created one that is exactly how you envisioned your perfect abode to be. Spacious, luxurious, centrally located, a Kshitij home does tick all the boxes of perfection, and effortlessly so! An evolved experience that goes beyond exhibitionism. Strategically located in the quaint and cozy locale of Matunga, Kshitij positions itself as the ideal choice for the doers, the achievers, with limitless potential, who want nothing but the best.

A home at Tridhaatu Kshitij welcomes you with expansive spaces, reflective of the endless possibilities that await you. This space has been carefully designed without compromise to create premium 3 BHK residences that open up to the sea breeze and pleasant windy skies. At only one residence per floor, our conscious decision to make Kshitij ooze discreet luxury is apparent and ideal.
